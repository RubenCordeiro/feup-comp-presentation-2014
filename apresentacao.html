# Autoanalyze ### A Tool for Working with Automata By *Duarte Duarte*, *Luís Cleto*, *Miguel Marques* and *Ruben Cordeiro* COMP/MIEIC/FEUP - 2013/2014
## What does it do?
* Reads automata from `dot` files * Performs several analyzes and operations * Provides a simple language for specifying the operations
### `Dot` Automata ![A dot file](images/dotfile.png)
## What have we already done?
* A `dot` grammar parser * An intermediate representation for `dot` graphs * A grammar for our `AutoScript` language

Grammars

  • dot grammar
  • AutoScript grammar

## What are we going to do?
* Implement automata analysis methods * Implement automata operations * Elaborate the `AutoScript` language and parser * support control flow constructs (`if`, `else`, `while`, `for`, ...) * multiple variable types (`dfa`, `nfa`, `bool`, ...)
## What would we also like to do?
* Extend transition labels to accept regular expressions * Convert automata to regular expressions * Methods to implement the automata in different languages * C/C++, Haskell, Java, ...
